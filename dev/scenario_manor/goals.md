# Scenario Goals

## Primary Goal
- Escape the cult stronghold alive.

## Secondary Goals
- Discover who (or what) brought you here.
- Learn the purpose of the ritual being prepared.

## Optional Objectives
- Collect at least three occult artifacts.
- Decipher the sigil on the torn page.
- Escape without losing any HP / sanity (if applicable).

Use this file to keep track of win / lose conditions and any
branching outcomes specific to this scenario.
